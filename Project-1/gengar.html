Gengar

Evolutionary line

The first pokemon in Gengars chain of evolution is Gastly (the left or top most pokemon). Gastly is a a little spherical blip of poisonous gas that plays pranks on people. It evolves into Haunter at level 25. Haunter (the center most pokemon) is kinda like Gastly but not spherical and posesses a pair disembodied hands. Haunter also likes playing pranks on people and it evolves into Gengar when it's traded. Gengar(!) the right or bottom most mass of dark purple awesomeness has a pair of red eyes, large pointed ears, and a wide sinister grin. Some other simular pokemon inclued Alakazam, Misdreavus, and Dusknoir.
