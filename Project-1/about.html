What We're All About

Places and Things

Every third Friday we meet up somewhere in Halle Library and discuss the direction of the club. Every now and then we will head out to Red's house for smash and pizza. Sundays we have a pokemon go group hunt(!) (See emails for more info) and we participate in the Pokemon TCG tournaments whenever they are available.

Goals

We exist to promote the greatness of Gengar! Eventually through the power of teamwork and friendship we will conquer the world! Okay really though we're here because we want to have a good time hanging out and playing pokemon, what more could you ask for? (Besides more Gengars!)
